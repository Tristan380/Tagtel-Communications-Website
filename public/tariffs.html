Tariffs and payment plans

Post-Paid Plans

Tagtel offers both pre-paid and post-paid services.

These allow the client unlimited airtime. The customer pays for the airtime used at the end of each month. Post-paid Plans are suitable for both individual and corporate clients, as long as they meet the heavy usage criterion laid down by Tagtel.

Pre-Paid Plans

The prepaid plan is primarily for those who want to be in control of what they spend on mobile phone calls. They purchase airtime and load it onto their phone. They can only make calls when their pre-paid account is in credit.

The advantages are that there is no contract, no credit checks, no waiting, and no bills. There is instant access to the network.

The Tagtel prepaid plan is arguably the most convenient in Zimbabwe at the moment, offering, as it does, good quality network connections and a range of value added services, such as credit transfer, remote recharge.
